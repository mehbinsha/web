Mutual Funds: Your Path to Financial Freedom

What are Mutual Funds?

A mutual fund is a professionally managed investment fund that pools money from many investors to invest in stocks, bonds, and other securities. It offers diversification and professional management even for small investors.

My Approach to Mutual Fund Selection:

  • Thorough research and due diligence.
  • Suitability to your risk profile and financial goals.
  • Selection based on excellent fund management teams and consistent performance.
  • Continuous monitoring and portfolio rebalancing.

Key Mutual Fund Categories:

  • Equity Funds (Invest in stocks)
  • Debt Funds (Invest in bonds and other fixed-income securities)
  • Hybrid Funds (Combination of equity and debt)
  • ELSS Funds (Helps save tax)
  • Index Funds (Tracks a specific index)
